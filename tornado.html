Description: A rapidly rotating column of air extending from a thunderstorm to the ground.

Recognition: Funnel cloud, dark sky, roaring sound, sudden hail.

Formation: The formation of a tornado is based off of a Supercell meets a trough in the right positioning and storms with hail, wind, rain, and thunder.

Impact: Can cause severe damage to buildings, uproot trees, and result in loss of life.

Scale: Enhanced Fujita Scale (EF Scale) from EF0 (weak) to EF5 (violent).

Safety: Seek shelter in a basement or interior room, away from windows.
