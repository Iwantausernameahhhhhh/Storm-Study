Description: A sudden shaking of the ground caused by the movement of tectonic plates.

Impact: Can cause structural damage, landslides, and tsunamis.

Safety: Drop, cover, and hold on during shaking. Stay away from windows and heavy objects.

Scale: Measured using the Richter scale or moment magnitude scale (Mw).

Recognition: Shaking ground, falling objects, and noise.

Formation: Occurs when stress on rocks exceeds their strength, causing a sudden release of energy.

Types: Shallow-focus, deep-focus, and surface waves.
