Description: An intense fire that creates its own wind system, often associated with wildfires.

Formation: Occurs when high temperatures and strong winds fuel a large fire, creating a self-sustaining wind system.

Impact: Can destroy property, harm wildlife, and pose health risks due to smoke inhalation.

Safety: Evacuate if instructed, avoid areas with heavy smoke, and wear protective masks if exposed to smoke.

Recognition: Large flames, intense heat, and strong winds feeding the fire.

Scale: No formal scale, but intensity is measured by fire size and wind strength.
