Ice Storm

← Back to Storms

Description: A type of winter storm characterized by freezing rain that coats surfaces with ice.

Impact: Can cause power outages, hazardous travel conditions, and damage to trees and structures.

Safety: Stay indoors, avoid travel, and prepare for power outages.

Scale: No formal scale, but severity can be categorized by ice accumulation (light, moderate, heavy).

Recognition: Freezing rain, ice accumulation on surfaces, and power outages.

Formation: Occurs when warm, moist air overrides a layer of cold air near the ground, causing rain to freeze on contact.

Types: Glaze ice, rime ice, and black ice.
