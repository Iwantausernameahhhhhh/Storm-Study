Description: A large rotating storm with high-speed winds that forms over warm waters in tropical areas.

Recognition: Strong winds, heavy rain, storm surge, and flooding.

Scale: Saffir-Simpson Hurricane Wind Scale (Category 1 to 5 based on wind speed).

Formation: Forms over warm ocean waters, requires low vertical wind shear, and moist air.

Impact: Can cause severe damage to buildings, infrastructure, and ecosystems; can lead to loss of life.

Safety: Evacuate if instructed, stay indoors, and avoid floodwaters.
