Description: A mass of rock, earth, or debris that moves down a slope due to gravity.

Impact: Can cause destruction to property, roads, and infrastructure; can lead to loss of life.

Safety: Stay away from steep slopes during heavy rain or seismic activity; evacuate if necessary.

Scale: No formal scale, but categorized by size and speed of movement.

Recognition: Sudden ground movement, falling rocks, and changes in landscape.

Formation: Triggered by heavy rainfall, earthquakes, volcanic activity, or human activities.
