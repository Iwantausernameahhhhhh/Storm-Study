Waterspout

üìñ Definitions

Description: A rotating column of air over water, similar to a tornado.

Formation: Develops over warm water under unstable atmospheric conditions, often during thunderstorms.

Impact: Can pose a threat to boats, coastal areas, and cause minor damage if it moves onshore.

Safety: Avoid boating during waterspout warnings and stay away from the water's edge.

Recognition: A funnel-shaped cloud extending from the sky to the water's surface.

Scale: No formal scale, but intensity is categorized by wind speed and size.
