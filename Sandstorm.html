Sandstorm

üìñ Definitions

Description: A strong windstorm that carries sand and dust through the air, reducing visibility and causing potential harm to people and property.

Formation: Sandstorms occur when strong winds lift loose sand and dirt from dry surfaces into the atmosphere.

Impact: Can reduce visibility, damage machinery, and pose health risks due to inhalation of dust particles.

Safety: Stay indoors, close windows and doors, and wear protective gear if outside.

Recognition: Dust clouds, reduced visibility, and strong winds.

Scale: No formal scale, but intensity can be categorized by wind speed and dust concentration.

Types: Dust storms, haboobs, and dust devils.
